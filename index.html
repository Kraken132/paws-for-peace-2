Our Story

Why We Help

We started Paws for Peace after witnessing countless stray dogs living on the streets of Bangkok — hungry, scared, and alone. Each dog has a soul full of love waiting to be given to the right family.

Our group volunteers every weekend, providing food, medical care, and love to rescues before finding them their forever homes. We believe every dog deserves a safe place to sleep and a family who truly cares.

Meet Our Rescues

Our rescue dogs come from all walks of life — some were abandoned, some were found injured, and others simply needed a second chance. Each one has been cared for by our dedicated volunteers, vaccinated, and prepared for their new life.

Whether you're looking for a playful pup, a gentle companion, or a loyal protector, we have a dog for every type of family. Browse our adoption page and open your heart to one of our beloved rescues.
